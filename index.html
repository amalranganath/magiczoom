magic zoom

jquery plugin to zoom the image galery

Browser support: FF, Chrome, IE7+, Safari, Opera Dependencies: juery1.9[, greensock tween library] Download: https://github.com/tanyaka/magiczoom/blob/master/js/

HTML: You need to create a DIV with thumbnails as usual IMG tags inside this div. Put the name of the preview image in the data-preview and name of the corresponding zoom image in the data-zoomed attributs respectivly. All the images (thumbnail, preview and zoom) should be in the same folder.

<div class="magic_zoom">
    <img src="img/thumb_moon_valey.jpg" width="42" height="30" data-preview="moon_valey400x220.jpg" data-zoomed="moon_valey1200x700.jpg" alt="Moon valey">
    <img src="img/thumb_MDRS_2.jpg" width="42" height="30" data-preview="MDRS_2_400x220.jpg" data-zoomed="MDRS_2_1200x700.jpg" alt="MDRS 2">
    <img src="img/thumb_MDRS_3.jpg" width="42" height="30" data-preview="MDRS_3_400x220.jpg" data-zoomed="MDRS_3_1200x700.jpg" alt="MDRS 3">
</div>

JS: $('.magic_zoom').magicZoom();

Options:

1. activeThumb, sets preview image starting from 0. Default value activeThumb = 0.
2. thumpPosition - the dirrection (vertical or horisontal) and positon (to the right, top or bottom) of the thumbnals compare to the preview image and postion of the zoomed image (to the left or to the bottom from the preview):
VR - (default) thumbnails verticaly right, zoom to the left
HB - thumbnails horizontaly at the bottom, zoom to the left
HT - thumbnails horizontaly at the top, zoom to the left
VRB - thumbnails verticaly right, zoom to the bottom
HTB - thumbnails horizontaly at the top, zoom to the bottom

For Example:

$('.magic_zoom').magicZoom({
  activeThumb: 2,
  thumpPosition: "HB"
});
